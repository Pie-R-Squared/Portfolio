My Top 5 Tourist Attractions

The London Eye

This attraction is located on the south bank of the River Thames in London. It is Europe's tallest cantilevered observation wheel and the most popular attraction in the UK. A full revolution of this wheel takes approximately 30 minutes, allowing tourists to see the stunning view of London city from a birds-eye view.

The Eiffel Tower

This attraction is located in Paris, France. The structure is made of iron and it spans three floors. Every year, around 7 million people are estimated to visit, making it the most visited monument that you have to pay for in the world.

The Taj Mahal

This attraction is an ivory-white marble mausoleum located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India. The complex structure took 22 years to build, with over 22,000 labourers working on the construction. Precious stones from various countries were brought for the building of the Taj Mahal.

The Burj Khalifa

This attraction is a skycraper in Dubai, UAE and it's the world's tallest structure with a height of 829.8m. It's composed of 163 floors with several open-air viewing decks and lounges, offering spectacular views of the UAE and the Persian Gulf.

The Chichen Itza

The Chichén Itzá attraction is a complex of Mayan ruins on Mexico's Yucatán Peninsula. It's one of the largest Mayan cities ever built and one of the most visited archaeological sites in Mexico today. Occupying an area of 4 square miles (10 square km), it is thought to have been a religious, military, political, and commercial centre that at its peak would have been home to 35,000 people.
